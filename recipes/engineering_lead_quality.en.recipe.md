## Scenario

Leaders juggle **code quality, decision records, meetings, and escalations**. Every skill stands alone; this page is only a compass.

## Suggested path (optional)

1. **PR culture:** Normalize `code_review` + `pr_description` snippets in your CONTRIBUTING guide.
2. **Architecture debates:** Use `technical_decision_record` before long meetings.
3. **Cross-team syncs:** Capture actions with `meeting_notes` after calls.
4. **Long-lived blockers:** Prefer structured `escalation_email` over chat pings.

## Note

If your process is mature, treat OpenSkill as **checklists and phrasing**—no need to replace existing tooling.
